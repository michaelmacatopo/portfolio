The Cottages at Pacific Trails

What is a cottage?

Our luxury cottages are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each cottages has canvas walls, a wooden floor, a roof dome that can be opened, and a private deck.

How are the cottages furnished?

Each cottages is furnished with a gas fireplace, king-sized bed with down quilt, comfy couch, dining table and chairs, mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker. Your luxury camping experience includes a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing wheather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
